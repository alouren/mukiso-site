Questions

Before you ask

What do you need from me to start?+

Your Amazon listing URL and five minutes on our intake form. We pull everything else from your listing, your reviews, and your competitors. If you have existing footage or brand assets, we can use them, but we don't need them.

Do I need to get on a call?+

Only if you want. Everything runs by email: pay, fill the intake form, receive your video. If you'd rather talk something through first, get in touch and we'll set it up.

What exactly do I receive?+

A 20-second MP4 in 1920x1080 (H.264), fully compliant with Amazon's video requirements, plus a thumbnail for your listing. Ready to upload the day you receive it.

How do revisions work?+

Two revision rounds are included with every video. Most clients approve the first cut without any revisions. If you have notes, send them by email and we'll deliver the updated cut.

Will the video pass Amazon's review?+

Yes. Compliance is built into our checklist: no superlative claims, no URLs, no QR codes, no watermarks, correct format and resolution. We've studied the rejection reasons so you don't have to.

I run an agency. Can I offer this to my clients?+

Yes. We partner with Amazon agencies and handle all production while you keep the client relationship. Get in touch and we'll set up the partnership.
